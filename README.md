## 🌍 Who I Am

> *"The most incomprehensible thing about the universe is that it is comprehensible."* — Einstein
> My job is to make that comprehension **computable** — and deploy it on a drone.

I'm a **physics-informed AI engineer** at [**Zeitview**](https://www.zeitview.com), building intelligent systems that help the world understand, maintain, and optimise its energy infrastructure — **from the sky**.

Drones fly. They collect terabytes of RGB, thermal, and LiDAR data over wind farms, solar fields, oil & gas pipelines, and real estate. My job starts after the drone lands — turning raw aerial data into **actionable asset intelligence** using the same mathematics that governs the physical systems being inspected.

Whether that's a **wind turbine wake deficit** modelled by the Jensen PDE (validated to <5% RMSE against 80 Vestas turbines at Horns Rev), a **grid curtailment** problem solved with MILP delivering 73% revenue uplift, or a **blade defect** classified through nonlinear scale-space operators — **every system I build starts with a governing equation**.
